About HON. JOHANA NGENO (NGONG)

HON. JOHANA NGENO (NGONG) is a dedicated political leader focused on improving infrastructure, education, and healthcare in Ngong. Known for his integrity and transparency, he champions community-driven development and empowerment for a better future.

HON. JOHANA NGENO

Member of Parliament
Emurua Dikirr Constituency

Visionary Leadership

With a steadfast commitment to public service, Hon. Johana Ngeno has dedicated his political career to uplifting the people of Emurua Dikirr Constituency through sustainable development initiatives and community empowerment programs.

"My mission is to create an inclusive, prosperous constituency where every citizen has access to quality education, healthcare, and economic opportunities."
Key Focus Areas:
  • Education & Youth Empowerment
  • Healthcare Accessibility
  • Infrastructure Development
  • Agricultural Modernization
Achievements:
  • Improved road networks
  • Enhanced educational facilities
  • Healthcare center upgrades
  • Youth employment programs
